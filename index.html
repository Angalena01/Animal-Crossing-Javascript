The New and Improved Animal Crossing 2.0
If you thought Animal Crossing was boring before, the new update could possibly change your mind. With new foods and some of your favorite characters from the past being added, animal crossing just might have been saved from the pit of boredom it was becoming.

New features are being added like recipes to cook, Brewsters Cafe, Gyroids are back, increased storage, and you can decorate your villagers houses. Many more things are being added as well but that comes with a fee of $25 for the dlc.
